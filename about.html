About Us

Big Four Experience. Advisory Firm Agility.

We founded Audit Readiness Advisors because we saw too many companies enter the audit unprepared — and we knew we could change that.

Our Story

Audit Readiness Advisors was built by audit professionals who spent years on the other side of the table — at Big Four firms — auditing companies across industries, borders, and accounting frameworks.

We saw the same patterns repeat: companies entering the audit with incomplete documentation, misapplied accounting standards, and control environments that couldn't withstand the scrutiny of a formal audit. The result was always the same — unexpected adjustments, delayed timelines, strained relationships, and eroded confidence.

We created this firm to solve that problem. Our mission is simple: help companies prepare for their financial audit with the same rigor and methodology that their auditors will apply — so that when the auditor arrives, the work is already done.

What Sets Us Apart

We're not generalist consultants. We're audit specialists — people who have spent careers inside audit rooms, evaluating evidence, testing controls, and assessing financial statements. This means we know exactly what your auditor will look for, what questions they'll ask, and what gaps will cause delays.

Big Four Pedigree

Our team's experience spans years at the world's leading audit firms. We apply the same methodology, rigor, and quality standards — without the bureaucracy or overhead.

Cross-Border Fluency

We operate natively across the US, Colombia, and Argentina. Our team is bilingual and experienced in multi-jurisdictional engagements spanning US GAAP, IFRS, and NIIF.

Partner-Level Attention
