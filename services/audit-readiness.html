Our Core Service

Audit Readiness Reviews & Mock Audits

Simulate the audit experience before your auditors arrive. Identify gaps, test your documentation, and face your audit with confidence.

The Problem

Too many companies enter their financial audit unprepared — leading to unexpected adjustments, delayed timelines, and strained relationships with auditors and stakeholders.

Common issues include incomplete supporting documentation, inconsistent application of accounting standards, unreconciled accounts, and a lack of understanding about what auditors will test. These problems compound under time pressure, turning audit season into a crisis rather than a process.

Our Solution

Our Audit Readiness Review is a rigorous, structured assessment modeled on Big Four audit methodology. We evaluate your financial statements, supporting documentation, accounting policies, and internal controls through the lens of your upcoming audit.

For companies that want a full simulation, our Mock Audit replicates the entire audit experience — document requests, management inquiries, walkthrough testing, and substantive procedures — providing a risk-free rehearsal that exposes weaknesses and prepares your team.

What You Get

  • Comprehensive review of financial statements against applicable standards (US GAAP, IFRS, NIIF)
  • Documentation completeness assessment and gap analysis
  • Walkthrough testing of key transaction cycles
  • Account reconciliation review
  • Disclosure adequacy assessment
  • Internal controls evaluation relevant to financial reporting
  • Prioritized findings report with risk ratings
  • Actionable remediation roadmap with timelines
  • Management letter-style communication of findings
  • Hands-on remediation support (optional engagement extension)

Who This Is For

This service is designed for:

  • Companies preparing for their first financial statement audit
  • Organizations that experienced audit issues in prior years
  • Venture-backed startups with growing audit requirements
  • Companies transitioning to new accounting frameworks
  • Multinational entities requiring coordinated audit preparation
  • PE-backed companies with rigorous investor reporting requirements

Our Process

Scoping & Planning

We define the engagement scope, understand your audit timeline, and tailor our approach to your specific risks.

Fieldwork

Our team performs substantive testing, walkthroughs, and documentation reviews mirroring actual audit procedures.

Reporting

We deliver a comprehensive findings report with prioritized issues and a clear remediation roadmap.

Remediation

Optional hands-on support to address findings and prepare your team for the formal audit.
