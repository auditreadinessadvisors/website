GAAP, IFRS & NIIF Technical Accounting Advisory

Navigate complex accounting standards with confidence. Expert guidance on US GAAP, IFRS, and NIIF for Colombia and Latin America — from initial assessment to implementation.

The Challenge

Accounting standards are becoming increasingly complex. Revenue recognition, lease accounting, financial instruments, business combinations, and consolidation requirements demand specialized knowledge that many in-house teams don't have the bandwidth to maintain.

Cross-border operations compound this challenge. Companies operating in the United States and Latin America must often navigate between US GAAP and IFRS/NIIF — two frameworks that share principles but diverge in critical areas. Misapplication of these standards leads to audit adjustments, regulatory scrutiny, and financial reporting delays.

How We Help

We provide hands-on technical accounting advisory across the full spectrum of US GAAP, IFRS, and NIIF requirements:

US GAAP Advisory

  • ASC 606 Revenue Recognition analysis and implementation
  • ASC 842 Lease Accounting transition and ongoing compliance
  • ASC 805 Business Combinations and purchase price allocation
  • ASC 810 Consolidation and variable interest entity assessment
  • ASC 326 Current Expected Credit Losses (CECL)
  • Fair value measurement and impairment testing

IFRS & NIIF Advisory

  • IFRS 16 / NIIF 16 Leases
