Financial Statement Diagnostics

A rigorous, line-by-line analysis of your financial statements to surface issues before your auditor does — protecting your credibility and accelerating the audit process.

The Problem

Financial statements are the foundation of every audit. When they contain misstatements, inconsistencies, or disclosure gaps, the consequences ripple through the entire engagement.

Auditors finding errors in your financial statements leads to audit adjustments, Management Letter comments, extended timelines, and — in the worst cases — restatements. These outcomes damage credibility with boards, investors, and lenders.

Our Approach

We apply Big Four-caliber analytical procedures to your financial statements, examining every material balance, transaction class, and disclosure against the applicable accounting framework. Our team identifies:

  • Potential material misstatements and errors in account balances
  • Inconsistencies between financial statement line items and supporting schedules
  • Classification errors impacting the quality of earnings and financial position
  • Disclosure deficiencies under US GAAP, IFRS, or NIIF requirements
  • Accounting policy elections that may be inconsistent or suboptimal
  • Presentation issues that could trigger auditor inquiries

What You Get

  • Detailed financial statement markup with identified issues annotated
  • Quantified impact analysis for each identified misstatement
  • Framework-specific guidance on required corrections
  • Disclosure checklist tailored to your framework and industry
  • Analytical review highlighting unusual balances and trends
  • Executive summary for CFO and board presentation
  • Corrective journal entries and restatement support where needed

Who This Is For

  • Companies preparing draft financial statements before audit fieldwork
  • CFOs who want an independent quality check on their reporting
  • Organizations adopting new accounting standards and need to validate compliance
  • Companies with complex transactions requiring careful accounting treatment
  • Multinational entities consolidating financial statements across jurisdictions
